Obsidian Agent Skills is a collection of structured skill definitions that teach AI coding assistants — including Claude Code, Codex, and OpenCode — to work fluently with Obsidian vault files. Each skill is a `SKILL.md` file that an agent loads automatically, giving it deep knowledge of Obsidian-specific syntax, file formats, and CLI commands without any manual prompting.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick your agent">
    These skills work with any [Agent Skills](https://agentskills.io/specification)-compatible agent: **Claude Code**, **Codex**, and **OpenCode** are all supported out of the box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the skills">
    Use the marketplace, `npx skills`, or a manual clone. See [Installation](/installation) for the exact command for your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start working in your vault">
    Open your Obsidian vault with your agent. The skills are auto-discovered — your agent will now understand wikilinks, `.base` files, `.canvas` files, and the Obsidian CLI with no extra prompting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
